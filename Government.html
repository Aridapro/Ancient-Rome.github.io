Leaders

The Roman leaders were different at times but the main and first leader was Caesar Augustus. He was born: September 23, 63 BCE, Palatine Hill, Rome, Italy. He died: August 19, 14 BCE, Nola, Italy. Augustus was vital for Ancient Rome as he did a huge expansion for ancient Rome. They had approximately 70 leaders in the past.
