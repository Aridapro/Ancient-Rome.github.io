In Ancient Rome the laws were only applied to the citizens. The laws were voted in by the general assemblies. If somebody broke the law the would most likely have to fight in the coliseum. (They could have to fight more than once.) Foreigners in ancient Rome had no protection. In ancient Rome if something happened to a foreigner, then no one would do anything about it.
